General struct for a channel in the Hodgkin-Huxley model.

The general expression of the channel is:

\[I_x = ar{g}_xm^p_xh^q_x(V_mV_x)\]

The gating variables $m$ and $h$ are are given by Œ±_m, Œ≤_m, Œ±_h and Œ≤_h respectively in the following way:

\[egin{aligned} rac{dm}{dt} = lpha(V_M)(1-m) - eta(V_M)m \ rac{dh}{dt} = lpha(V_M)(1-h) - eta(V_M)h nd{aligned}\]

LaskaML.hh.ab_generalized ‚Äî Function
ab_generalized(V, p)

The generalized form of Œ± and Œ≤.

function ab_generalized(V, p)
    A, B, C, D, F, H = p
    (A + B * V) / (C + H * exp((V + D) / F))
end
source

A generalized version of the Œ±/Œ≤ functions as per Nelson, M.E. (2004)

Calculated as:

\[\alpha(V) = \frac{A + BV}{C+Hexp(\frac{V+D}{F})}\]
